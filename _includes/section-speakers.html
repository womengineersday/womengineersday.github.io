Denise Koessler Gosnell, PhD

Data Scientist, PokitDok

Dr. Gosnell is a go-getter who pursues life with passion and enthusiasm. During her time at the University of Tennessee, she was involved in two start-ups, founded the Systers group, and was awarded TA of the year for the department of computer science in 2013. Her PhD research has been featured at conferences from London to San Diego. She currently works for a Silicon Valley start-up called PokitDok.
