WomEngineers Day is a one-day mini-conference where professional engineers and business partners provide their own expertise on both professional and personal topics related to the career and life choices they have encountered in their careers to University of Tennessee COE students planning their own careers.

In addition to highlighting successful women in industry, this mini-conference is a rare opportunity for both male and female engineering candidates alike to attend a full-scale professional conference focused on the topics BOTH will need in identifying, and ultimately establishing, a successful career in business and industry. WomEngineers Day will include speakers, discussion panels, and breakout sessions to provide each student with those professional and personal skills needed which can never be taught within even the best engineering curriculums. This is a unique opportunity for you to gain advice and mentorship from successful executives in both the corporate and private sectors that you may never have the opportunity to meet otherwise. Quite frankly, you may look back on this event as the most important learning opportunity of your scholastic experience.
