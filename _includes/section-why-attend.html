What is WomEngineers Day?

  • Go beyond the classroom by hearing about professional and personal topics related to career and life choices.
  • Listen from a diverse group of speakers from different career positions, educational backgrounds, and career paths.
  • Network with other students, leaders in industry, and professors from all over the United States.

Who is WomEngineers Day for?

  • Any high school student, community college student, or university student looking to expand their understanding of a career.
  • While the conference is called WomEngineers Day, we welcome and encourage men to attend! The topics that will be covered affect all engineers, no matter your gender.
